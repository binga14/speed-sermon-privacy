Privacy Policy

for Speed Sermon by Shuhat

This Privacy Policy explains how Shuhat (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use Speed Sermon (the “App”).

If you do not collect some of the data described below, remove those parts to match your real behavior.

1. Information we collect

A) Information you provide

  • Account / profile info (if you create an account): name, email, phone (optional), profile photo (optional).
  • Support messages: information you send when contacting us.

B) Information collected automatically

  • Device & app data: device model, OS version, app version, language, time zone, identifiers (as available).
  • Usage data: screens viewed, taps, crashes, performance logs, and diagnostic data.
  • Approximate location (only if you enable it / grant permission): approximate region/city-level data.

C) Content you create in the app

  • Any content you upload or generate (for example: images, text, files) depending on what your app does.

2. How we use information

  • Provide and improve the App and its features
  • Authenticate users and maintain security
  • Fix bugs, monitor crashes, and improve performance
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing of information

We may share information in these situations:

  • Service providers that help operate the App (hosting, analytics, crash reporting, customer support).
  • Legal requests if required by law, court order, or to protect rights/safety.
  • Business transfers (merger, acquisition, asset sale) — you will be notified if required.

Common third-party services (edit to match your app)

  • Expo (if you use Expo services)
  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase (Auth / Analytics / Crashlytics) (if used)
  • Sentry (crash/error monitoring) (if used)

If you don’t use any of these, remove them.

4. Data retention

We keep information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, unless a longer retention period is required by law. We delete or anonymize data when it’s no longer needed.

5. Your choices & rights

  • You can usually manage permissions (camera, storage, location) in your device settings.
  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us.

6. Data deletion request

To request deletion, email us at: support@speedsermon.com with the subject line Data Deletion Request. Include your account identifier (for example: email/phone used in the app).

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. International users

If you use the App from outside your country, your information may be processed in other countries where our service providers operate.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date above.
